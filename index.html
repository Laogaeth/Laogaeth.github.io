About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer hailing from Portugal. My first interaction with this field was back in 2013 by tinkering with CSS on Tumblrm granted I didn't know what I was doing back then but it left a small mark on me.

It's hard for me to explain why take up programming, it always has been a part of my life even if not directly, always had a passion for tech and I'm excited to now have the opportunity to work within it, if someone were to tell me couple years back that I would enjoy spending hours in front of a computer looking at lines of text I would have laughed, but here we are.

At the moment I'm looking to break into this career path and hopefully stick with a company for years to come.
